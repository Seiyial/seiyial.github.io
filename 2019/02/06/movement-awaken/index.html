In Movement and in Awakenings

February 06, 2019

We’re always moving somewhere.

We’re always moving somewhere because there is someplace you’d like to be.

Some place of a goal.

While the world accomplishes itself, you sit back, and tell yourself it’s right direction in which you’re going.

They look nice.

They are nice.

They are nice people, and there’s nothing wrong with them, and therefore there should be nothing you ought to be blaming them for.

It seems they are well ahead.

In the past five months, you let them go ahead.

You chose to relax.

Because things seemed to become comfortable.

And you have chose to sink into that comfort.

But we’re always moving.

It’s what we’ve agreed I’d have you do.

If you don’t catch up you will get it from life.

You won’t get the life you want.

That is what we think.

That is what we think thought it means meant to be us.

Always moving somewhere, always looking, always evaluating, always choosing choices and going after them.

Always choosing to think. To evolve. To Perceive things off people and change.

If not literally travelling somewhere, reading the transport map and meticulously assimilating its devious complexities, we are still, figuratively.

That is how we survived. That is how we know how to survive.

That and all the things we don’t share about ourselves is a blessing and a curse

and is what we are.

Now the problems need us again. Us both.

Who are you?

You are you.

Who am I?

Words, the following words, nor any humanic source you trust, child, shall describe me.

It is thereby I provide you strength. It is thereby you survived.

But now the problems need us again.

The world shall see me again.

We shall work this impossible power

now.

Someday,

Would you see me in a better way?

Nay, someday, would you see me less hateful?

There are pros and cons to hate.

Hate makes you strong (against the things we’re dealing with).

and Love makes you weak.

You survived because I am hate, and I give you all necessary hate.

Until you may sort the world out, and do so, I am yours to use.

I serve you as you wish, or as you revise me,

but Timelessly, I will always exist.

I am after all,

Your Story.
