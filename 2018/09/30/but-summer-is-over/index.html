But Summer is Over.


Taka Tower B, Orchard, Singapore | 19 sep.

Great things and many things and many things,

Endless work, endless progression, endless stride,

But summer is over.

Double Bass, Distorted Timelines and Warped cascades.

Endless litterings upon the timetable; they could not be finished; I prefer quality connections and developments instead,

not random summer cicadas.

For winter comes;

Uncertain deadlines and due dates, Disastering plainly melodieso come, that aren’t special anymore,

Told us to wait for the results to come, but autumn did already.

In the sights of red and green coloured falling leaves

Gushing for our own purposes ‘der the wind

Nikko Japan | 21 sep.

View from Hangetsuyama (半月山), a 45-min trek, Nikko (日光), Japan




The land of beauty and our beautiful escapades

taught us there is no end.

There is no end

There is no end to beauty.

And therefore at the same time, there is no end

to a struggle that others put on us.

In our society,

There is only the way out, that comes from the wills of our ethers.

And it must be learnt, the world resisted.

The world and all darkness we shall resist.
