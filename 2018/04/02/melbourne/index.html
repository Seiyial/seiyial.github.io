In the city of Melbourne

A goal hath brewed.

That goal was simple.

It is a goal of freedom.

It is a goal that unleashes the strength in freedom.

Melbourne is beautiful; it is a creation of the human cultures, of past and present.

If the work of coding were all about coding and not about this, then coding isn’t beautiful.

Yes; coding is ultimately about whom the code is meant for; what the code helps to do in the world.

Some men bear advances, but their stories are too different from mine.

It is thus I owe it to myself, and my having seen this great city,

to respect what this great city has taught me.

I will walk down another path, however less comfortable that would be.

I will do things that do ultimately deserve my spirit.

Melbourne is beautiful.

Freedom is beautiful.

Culture is beautiful.

There is a world to be served.

And seize it I will.
